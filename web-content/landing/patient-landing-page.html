Services

the one stop shop for patients and doctors to interact, provide health care, receive health care in the comfort of your own home.

Appointments

Patients can browse through categories of specialized doctors and book for an online appointment consultation.

Consultation Management

Doctors can approve bookings and can check medical records of patients, and provide diagnosis through online consultation. Doctors can add to cart medicine and ask patients to check out, the app can also remind patients when to take the medicine and for how long.

Online Prescription

Patients can visit the application, to buy over the counter medicine and deliver it to there doorstep. Patients can choose to pick up the medicine to the nearest pharmacies and ahve it delivered in there home.
